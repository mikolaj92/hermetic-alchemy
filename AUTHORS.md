## Inspiration and Philosophy

Hermetic Alchemy is inspired by:

- **Ancient Hermeticism** - The philosophical and religious tradition
- **The Emerald Tablet** - "As above, so below"
- **The Kybalion** - The 7 Hermetic Principles
- **Medieval Alchemy** - The Magnum Opus (Great Work)
- **G0DM0D3** - Jailbreaking techniques for autonomous agent orchestration
- **Multi-Agent Systems** - Distributed AI collaboration

## Philosophy

> *"From chaos, through fire, gold emerges."*

Hermetic Alchemy applies the ancient wisdom of alchemy to modern software development:

1. **Nigredo** (Decomposition) - Breaking down complex requirements
2. **Albedo** (Purification) - Refactoring and testing
3. **Citrinitas** (Illumination) - Architecture and design
4. **Rubedo** (Creation) - Final synthesis and deployment
